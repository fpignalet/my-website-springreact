GAME OVER

  • move with arrows or wasd or mouse or touch
  • shoot with space (with arrows / wasd) or automatic (with mouse / touch)
  • loose 20 life pts (progress bar) each time an ennemy reach left side
  • win 1 life pt (progress bar) each time an ennemy is killed
